DIFLUCORTOLONE VALERATE

Indications

 

severe inflammatory skin disorders such as eczemas unresponsive to less potent corticosteroids; high strength (0.3%), short-term treatment of severe exacerbations; psoriasis, see notes above

Cautions

 

see notes above

Contra-indications

 

see notes above

Side-effects

 

see notes above

Dose

 

Apply thinly 1–2 times daily for up to 4 weeks (0.1% preparations) or 2 weeks (0.3% preparations), reducing strength as condition responds; max. 60 g of 0.3% per week
