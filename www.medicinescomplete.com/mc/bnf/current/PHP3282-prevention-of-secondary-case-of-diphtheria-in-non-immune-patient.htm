Prevention of secondary case of diphtheria in non-immune patient

Erythromycin(1) 500 mg every 6 hours for 7 days; child up to 2 years 125 mg every 6 hours, 2–8 years 250 mg every 6 hours

Treat for further 10 days if nasopharyngeal swabs positive after first 7 days' treatment. For immunisation against diphtheria see section 14.4

  1. Where erythromycin is suggested another macrolide (e.g. azithromycin or clarithromycin) may be used
