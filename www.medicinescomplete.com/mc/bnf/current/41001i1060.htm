Posaconazole

Posaconazole has the following interaction information:

Apixabanavoidance of posaconazole advised by manufacturer of apixaban
Atazanavir posaconazole increases plasma concentration of atazanavir
Atorvastatinincreased risk of myopathy when posaconazole given with atorvastatin (avoid concomitant use)
Carbamazepineplasma concentration of posaconazole possibly reduced by carbamazepine
Ciclosporin posaconazole inhibits metabolism of ciclosporin (increased plasma concentration)
Cimetidineplasma concentration of posaconazole reduced by cimetidine
Dronedaroneavoidance of posaconazole advised by manufacturer of dronedarone
Efavirenzplasma concentration of posaconazole reduced by efavirenz
Everolimus posaconazole possibly increases plasma concentration of everolimus—manufacturer of everolimus advises avoid concomitant use
Glipizide posaconazole possibly enhances hypoglycaemic effect of glipizide
Histamine H2-antagonistsmanufacturer of posaconazole advises avoid concomitant use with histamine H2-antagonists (plasma concentration of posaconazole possibly reduced)
Lapatinibavoidance of posaconazole advised by manufacturer of lapatinib
Midazolam posaconazole increases plasma concentration of midazolam (risk of prolonged sedation)
Phenobarbitalplasma concentration of posaconazole possibly reduced by phenobarbital
Phenytoinplasma concentration of posaconazole reduced by phenytoin
Proton Pump Inhibitorsmanufacturer of posaconazole advises avoid concomitant use with proton pump inhibitors (plasma concentration of posaconazole possibly reduced)
Ranolazine posaconazole possibly increases plasma concentration of ranolazine—manufacturer of ranolazine advises avoid concomitant use
Rifabutin posaconazole increases plasma concentration of rifabutin (also plasma concentration of posaconazole reduced)
Rifampicinplasma concentration of posaconazole reduced by rifampicin
Rivaroxabanavoidance of posaconazole advised by manufacturer of rivaroxaban
Simvastatinincreased risk of myopathy when posaconazole given with simvastatin (avoid concomitant use)
Sirolimus posaconazole possibly increases plasma concentration of sirolimus
Tacrolimus posaconazole increases plasma concentration of tacrolimus (consider reducing dose of tacrolimus)Interactions do not generally apply to tacrolimus used topically; risk of facial flushing and skin irritation with alcohol consumption (see section 13.5.3) does not apply to tacrolimus taken systemically
Telaprevirplasma concentration of posaconazole possibly increased by telaprevir (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Vinblastine posaconazole possibly inhibits metabolism of vinblastine (increased risk of neurotoxicity)
Vincristine posaconazole possibly inhibits metabolism of vincristine (increased risk of neurotoxicity)

Posaconazole belongs to Antifungals, Triazole and will have the following interactions:

Amphotericin triazoles possibly antagonise effects of amphotericin Close monitoring required with concomitant administration of nephrotoxic drugs or cytotoxics
Artemether with Lumefantrineavoidance of triazoles advised by manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine
Atorvastatinpossible increased risk of myopathy when triazoles given with atorvastatin
Ergotamine and Methysergideincreased risk of ergotism when triazoles given with ergotamine and methysergide—avoid concomitant use
Fentanyl triazoles possibly increase plasma concentration of fentanyl
Pimozideincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when triazoles given with pimozide—avoid concomitant use
Quetiapine triazoles possibly increase plasma concentration of quetiapine—manufacturer of quetiapine advises avoid concomitant use
Reboxetineavoidance of triazoles advised by manufacturer of reboxetine
Rifabutin triazoles possibly increase plasma concentration of rifabutin (increased risk of uveitis—reduce rifabutin dose)
Saquinavir triazoles possibly increase plasma concentration of saquinavir

Posaconazole belongs to Antifungals but Antifungals has no interactions information.
