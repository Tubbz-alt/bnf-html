Telaprevir

Telaprevir has the following interaction information:

Alfuzosinmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with alfuzosin
Amiodaronemanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with amiodarone (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)Amiodarone has a long half-life; there is a potential for drug interactions to occur for several weeks (or even months) after treatment with it has been stopped
Amlodipine telaprevir increases plasma concentration of amlodipine (consider reducing dose of amlodipine)
Atazanavirplasma concentration of telaprevir possibly reduced by atazanavir , also plasma concentration of atazanavir possibly increased
Atorvastatinmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with atorvastatin
Bosentanplasma concentration of telaprevir possibly reduced by bosentan , also plasma concentration of bosentan possibly increased
Budesonide telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of inhaled and intranasal budesonide
Carbamazepinemanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with carbamazepine
Ciclosporinplasma concentration of both drugs increased when telaprevir given with ciclosporin (reduce dose of ciclosporin)
Clarithromycinplasma concentration of both drugs possibly increased when telaprevir given with clarithromycin (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Colchicine telaprevir possibly increases risk of colchicine toxicity—suspend or reduce dose of colchicine (avoid concomitant use in hepatic or renal impairment)
Dabigatran Etexilate telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of dabigatran etexilate
Darunavirmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with darunavir
Dexamethasoneplasma concentration of telaprevir possibly reduced by dexamethasone
Digoxin telaprevir increases plasma concentration of digoxin
Diltiazemmanufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with diltiazem
Disopyramidemanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with disopyramide (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Domperidone telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of domperidone—manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use
Efavirenzplasma concentration of telaprevir reduced by efavirenz—increase dose of telaprevir
Ergot Alkaloidsmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with ergot alkaloids
Erythromycinplasma concentration of both drugs possibly increased when telaprevir given with erythromycin (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)Interactions do not apply to small amounts of erythromycin used topically
Ethinylestradiol telaprevir possibly reduces plasma concentration of ethinylestradiol—manufacturer of telaprevir advises additional contraceptive precautions
Felodipinemanufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with felodipine
Flecainidemanufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with flecainide (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Fluticasone telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of inhaled and intranasal fluticasone
Fosamprenavirmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with fosamprenavir Fosamprenavir is a prodrug of amprenavir
Itraconazole telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of itraconazole
Ketoconazoleplasma concentration of both drugs possibly increased when telaprevir given with ketoconazole (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Lidocainemanufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with intravenous lidocaine Interactions less likely when lidocaine used topically
Lopinavirmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with lopinavir In combination with ritonavir as Kaletra® (ritonavir is present to inhibit lopinavir metabolism and increase plasma-lopinavir concentration)—see also Ritonavir
Methadonemanufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with methadone (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Midazolam telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of midazolam (risk of prolonged sedation—avoid concomitant use of oral midazolam)
Nicardipinemanufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with nicardipine
Nifedipinemanufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with nifedipine
Phenobarbitalmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with phenobarbital
Phenytoinmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with phenytoin
Pimozidemanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with pimozide
Posaconazole telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of posaconazole (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Propafenonemanufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with propafenone (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Rifabutinmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with rifabutin
Rifampicin telaprevir significantly reduces plasma concentration of rifampicin—avoid concomitant use
Ritonavirplasma concentration of telaprevir possibly reduced by ritonavir
Salmeterolmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with salmeterol (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Sildenafilmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with sildenafil
Simvastatinmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with simvastatin
Sirolimusplasma concentration of both drugs increased when telaprevir given with sirolimus (reduce dose of sirolimus)
Sotalolmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with sotalol (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
St John's Wortmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with St John's wort
Tacrolimusplasma concentration of both drugs increased when telaprevir given with tacrolimus (reduce dose of tacrolimus)Interactions do not generally apply to tacrolimus used topically; risk of facial flushing and skin irritation with alcohol consumption (see section 13.5.3) does not apply to tacrolimus taken systemically
Tadalafilmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with high doses of tadalafil—consult product literature
Telithromycinplasma concentration of both drugs possibly increased when telaprevir given with telithromycin (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Tenofovir telaprevir increases plasma concentration of tenofovir
Trazodone telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of trazodone
Vardenafilmanufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with vardenafil
Verapamilmanufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with verapamil
Voriconazole telaprevir possibly affects plasma concentration of voriconazole (possible increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)
Warfarin telaprevir possibly affects plasma concentration of warfarin

Telaprevir belongs to Antivirals but Antivirals has no interactions information.
