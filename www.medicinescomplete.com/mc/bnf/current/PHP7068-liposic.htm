    CARBOMERS(Polyacrylic acid)

    Note

     

    Synthetic high molecular weight polymers of acrylic acid cross-linked with either allyl ethers of sucrose or allyl ethers of pentaerithrityl

    Indications

     

    dry eyes including keratoconjunctivitis sicca, unstable tear film

    Dose

    • Apply 3–4 times daily or as required

    Sub-sections

    • Clinitas Gel®
    • GelTears®
