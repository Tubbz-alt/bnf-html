NICOTINAMIDE

Indications

 

see under preparation

Cautions

 

avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes (including nose and mouth); reduce frequency of application if excessive dryness, irritation or peeling

Side-effects

 

dry skin, pruritus, erythema, burning, irritation

Sub-sections

Nicam® (Dermal)

Gel, nicotinamide 4%, net price 60 g = £7.10

Excipients none as listed in section 13.1.3.

Dose inflammatory acne vulgaris, apply twice daily; reduce to once daily or on alternate days if irritation occurs
