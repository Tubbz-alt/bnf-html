Ergot Alkaloids

Ergot Alkaloids has the following interaction information:

Atazanavirplasma concentration of ergot alkaloids possibly increased by atazanavir—avoid concomitant use
Bocepreviravoidance of ergot alkaloids advised by manufacturer of boceprevir
Efavirenzincreased risk of ergotism when ergot alkaloids given with efavirenz—avoid concomitant use
Telapreviravoidance of ergot alkaloids advised by manufacturer of telaprevir
Ticagrelorplasma concentration of ergot alkaloids possibly increased by ticagrelor

Ergometrine belongs to Ergot Alkaloids and has the following interaction information:

Halothaneeffects of ergometrine on the parturient uterus reduced by halothane

Ergotamine and Methysergide belongs to Ergot Alkaloids and has the following interaction information:

Almotriptanincreased risk of vasospasm when ergotamine and methysergide given with almotriptan (avoid ergotamine and methysergide for 6 hours after almotriptan, avoid almotriptan for 24 hours after ergotamine and methysergide)
Antifungals, Imidazoleincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with imidazoles—avoid concomitant use
Antifungals, Triazoleincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with triazoles—avoid concomitant use
Beta-blockersincreased peripheral vasoconstriction when ergotamine and methysergide given with beta-blockers Since systemic absorption may follow topical application of beta-blockers to the eye the possibility of interactions, in particular, with drugs such as verapamil should be borne in mind
Cimetidineincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with cimetidine—avoid concomitant use
Eletriptanincreased risk of vasospasm when ergotamine and methysergide given with eletriptan (avoid ergotamine and methysergide for 24 hours after eletriptan, avoid eletriptan for 24 hours after ergotamine and methysergide)
Fosamprenavirincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with fosamprenavir—avoid concomitant useFosamprenavir is a prodrug of amprenavir
Frovatriptanincreased risk of vasospasm when ergotamine and methysergide given with frovatriptan (avoid ergotamine and methysergide for 24 hours after frovatriptan, avoid frovatriptan for 24 hours after ergotamine and methysergide)
Indinavirincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with indinavir—avoid concomitant use
Macrolidesincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with macrolides—avoid concomitant useSee also Telithromycin
Naratriptanincreased risk of vasospasm when ergotamine and methysergide given with naratriptan (avoid ergotamine and methysergide for 24 hours after naratriptan, avoid naratriptan for 24 hours after ergotamine and methysergide)
Nelfinavirincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with nelfinavir—avoid concomitant use
Reboxetinepossible risk of hypertension when ergotamine and methysergide given with reboxetine
Ritonavirincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with ritonavir—avoid concomitant use
Rizatriptanincreased risk of vasospasm when ergotamine and methysergide given with rizatriptan (avoid ergotamine and methysergide for 6 hours after rizatriptan, avoid rizatriptan for 24 hours after ergotamine and methysergide)
Saquinavirincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with saquinavir—avoid concomitant use
Sumatriptanincreased risk of vasospasm when ergotamine and methysergide given with sumatriptan (avoid ergotamine and methysergide for 6 hours after sumatriptan, avoid sumatriptan for 24 hours after ergotamine and methysergide)
Sympathomimeticsincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with sympathomimetics
Telithromycinincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with telithromycin—avoid concomitant use
Tetracyclinesincreased risk of ergotism when ergotamine and methysergide given with tetracyclines
Zolmitriptanincreased risk of vasospasm when ergotamine and methysergide given with zolmitriptan (avoid ergotamine and methysergide for 6 hours after zolmitriptan, avoid zolmitriptan for 24 hours after ergotamine and methysergide)

Methysergide belongs to Ergot Alkaloids but has no specific interaction information.
