HYDROCORTISONE

Indications

 

mild inflammatory skin disorders such as eczemas (but for over-the-counter preparations, see below); nappy rash (see also section 13.2.2)

Cautions

 

see notes above

Contra-indications

 

see notes above

Side-effects

 

see notes above

Dose

 

Apply thinly 1–2 times daily
