Velaglucerase alfa

(VPRIV®)

Intermittent in Sodium chloride 0.9%

Reconstitute each 400–unit vial with 4.3 mL water for injections to produce a 100 units/mL solution; dilute requisite dose in 100 mL infusion fluid; give over 60 minutes through a 0.22 micron filter; start infusion within 24 hours of reconstitution
