Voriconazole

(Vfend®)

Intermittent in Glucose 5% or Sodium chloride 0.9%

Reconstitute each 200 mg with 19 mL water for injections to produce a 10 mg/mL solution; dilute dose in infusion fluid to concentration of 0.5–5 mg/mL; give at a rate not exceeding 3 mg/kg/hour
