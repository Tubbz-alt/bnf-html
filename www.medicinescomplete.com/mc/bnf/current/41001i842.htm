Nelfinavir

Nelfinavir has the following interaction information:

Alfuzosinmanufacturer of nelfinavir advises avoid concomitant use with alfuzosin
Amiodaroneincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when nelfinavir given with amiodarone—avoid concomitant useAmiodarone has a long half-life; there is a potential for drug interactions to occur for several weeks (or even months) after treatment with it has been stopped
Apixabanavoidance of nelfinavir advised by manufacturer of apixaban
Aripiprazole nelfinavir possibly inhibits metabolism of aripiprazole (reduce dose of aripiprazole)
Artemether with Lumefantrinecaution with nelfinavir advised by manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine
Atorvastatinpossible increased risk of myopathy when nelfinavir given with atorvastatin
Azithromycin nelfinavir increases plasma concentration of azithromycin (increased risk of toxicity)
Budesonide nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of budesonide (including inhaled and intranasal budesonide)
Cabazitaxelavoidance of nelfinavir advised by manufacturer of cabazitaxel
Carbamazepineplasma concentration of nelfinavir possibly reduced by carbamazepine
Ciclosporin nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of ciclosporin
Colchicine nelfinavir possibly increases risk of colchicine toxicity—suspend or reduce dose of colchicine (avoid concomitant use in hepatic or renal impairment)
Darifenacinavoidance of nelfinavir advised by manufacturer of darifenacin
Eletriptan nelfinavir increases plasma concentration of eletriptan (risk of toxicity)—avoid concomitant use
Eplerenone nelfinavir increases plasma concentration of eplerenone—avoid concomitant use
Ergotamine and Methysergideincreased risk of ergotism when nelfinavir given with ergotamine and methysergide—avoid concomitant use
Esomeprazoleplasma concentration of nelfinavir possibly reduced by esomeprazole—manufacturer of esomeprazole advises avoid concomitant use
Etravirineplasma concentration of nelfinavir possibly increased by etravirine—avoid concomitant use
Everolimus nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of everolimus—manufacturer of everolimus advises avoid concomitant use
Fesoterodinemanufacturer of fesoterodine advises dose reduction when nelfinavir given with fesoterodine—consult fesoterodine product literature
Fluticasone nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of inhaled and intranasal fluticasone
Indinavircombination of nelfinavir with indinavir may increase plasma concentration of either drug (or both)
Ivabradine nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of ivabradine—avoid concomitant use
Lopinavir nelfinavir reduces plasma concentration of lopinavir , also plasma concentration of active metabolite of nelfinavir increasedIn combination with ritonavir as Kaletra® (ritonavir is present to inhibit lopinavir metabolism and increase plasma-lopinavir concentration)—see also Ritonavir
Maraviroc nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of maraviroc (consider reducing dose of maraviroc)
Methadone nelfinavir reduces plasma concentration of methadone
Midazolam nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of midazolam (risk of prolonged sedation—avoid concomitant use of oral midazolam)
Oestrogens nelfinavir accelerates metabolism of oestrogens (reduced contraceptive effect—see section 7.3.1)Interactions of combined oral contraceptives may also apply to combined contraceptive patches and vaginal rings, see section 7.3.1
Omeprazoleplasma concentration of nelfinavir reduced by omeprazole—avoid concomitant use
Paclitaxel nelfinavir increases plasma concentration of paclitaxel
Pazopanibavoidance of nelfinavir advised by manufacturer of pazopanib
Phenobarbitalplasma concentration of nelfinavir possibly reduced by phenobarbital
Phenytoin nelfinavir reduces plasma concentration of phenytoin
Pimozide nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of pimozide (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias—avoid concomitant use)
Progestogens nelfinavir possibly reduces contraceptive effect of progestogens Interactions of combined oral contraceptives may also apply to combined contraceptive patches and vaginal rings, see section 7.3.1. For further information on interactions of oral progestogen-only contraceptives, see also section 7.3.2.1; parenteral progestogen-only contraceptives, see also section 7.3.2.2; the intra-uterine progestogen-only device, see also section 7.3.2.3; hormonal emergency contraception, see also section 7.3.5
Quetiapine nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of quetiapine—manufacturer of quetiapine advises avoid concomitant use
Quinine nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of quinine (increased risk of toxicity)
Ranolazine nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of ranolazine—manufacturer of ranolazine advises avoid concomitant use
Rifabutin nelfinavir increases plasma concentration of rifabutin (halve dose of rifabutin)
Rifampicinplasma concentration of nelfinavir significantly reduced by rifampicin—avoid concomitant use
Ritonavircombination of nelfinavir with ritonavir may increase plasma concentration of either drug (or both)
Rivaroxabanavoidance of nelfinavir advised by manufacturer of rivaroxaban
Rosuvastatinpossible increased risk of myopathy when nelfinavir given with rosuvastatin—manufacturer of rosuvastatin advises avoid concomitant use
Salmeterolmanufacturer of nelfinavir advises avoid concomitant use with salmeterol
Saquinavir nelfinavir increases plasma concentration of saquinavir—manufacturer of saquinavir advises avoid concomitant use
Sildenafil nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of sildenafil—reduce initial dose of sildenafil
Simvastatinincreased risk of myopathy when nelfinavir given with simvastatin (avoid concomitant use)
Solifenacin nelfinavir increases plasma concentration of solifenacin
St John's Wortplasma concentration of nelfinavir reduced by St John's wort—avoid concomitant use
Tacrolimus nelfinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of tacrolimus Interactions do not generally apply to tacrolimus used topically; risk of facial flushing and skin irritation with alcohol consumption (see section 13.5.3) does not apply to tacrolimus taken systemically
Tadalafilmanufacturer of nelfinavir advises caution with tadalafil
Telithromycinavoidance of concomitant nelfinavir in severe renal and hepatic impairment advised by manufacturer of telithromycin
Tolterodineavoidance of nelfinavir advised by manufacturer of tolterodine
Vardenafilmanufacturer of nelfinavir advises caution with vardenafil

Nelfinavir belongs to Antivirals but Antivirals has no interactions information.
