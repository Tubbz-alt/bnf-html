CHLORAMPHENICOL

Indications

 

see notes above

Pregnancy

 

avoid unless essential—no information on topical use but risk of ‘neonatal grey-baby syndrome’ with oral use in third trimester

Breast-feeding

 

avoid unless essential—theoretical risk of bone-marrow toxicity

Side-effects

 

transient stinging; see also notes above

Dose

  • See Administration in notes above
