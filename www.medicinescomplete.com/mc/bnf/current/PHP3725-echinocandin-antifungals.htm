5.2.4 Echinocandin antifungals

The echinocandin antifungals include anidulafungin, caspofungin and micafungin. They are only active against Aspergillus spp. and Candida spp.; however, anidulafungin and micafungin are not used for the treatment of aspergillosis. Echinocandins are not effective against fungal infections of the CNS. For the role of echinocandin antifungals in the prevention and systemic treatment of fungal infections, see Treatment of Fungal Infections.
