CORTICOSTEROIDS

Indications

 

oral and perioral lesions

Contra-indications

 

untreated oral infection

Side-effects

 

occasional exacerbation of local infection; thrush or other candidal infections

Sub-sections

Hydrocortisone (Non-proprietary)

Mucoadhesive buccal tablets (= oromucosal tablets), hydrocortisone 2.5 mg (as sodium succinate). Net price 20 = £4.06

Dose adult and child over 12 years, 1 lozenge 4 times daily, allowed to dissolve slowly in the mouth in contact with the ulcer; child under 12 years, only on medical advice

Dental prescribing on NHS

 

May be prescribed as Hydrocortisone Oromucosal Tablets
