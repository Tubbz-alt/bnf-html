FERROUS SULFATE WITH ASCORBIC ACID

Additional information

 

interactions (Ascorbic acid,Iron).

Indications

 

prophylaxis and treatment of iron deficiency anaemia and treatment of concurrent vitamin C deficiency

Cautions

 

interactions: Appendix 1 (iron, vitamins)

Side-effects

 

see notes above

Dose

  • adult and child over 12 years, 1 tablet daily before food
