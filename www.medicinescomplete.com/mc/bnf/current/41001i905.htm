St John's Wort

St John's Wort has the following interaction information:

5HT1-receptor Agonistsincreased serotonergic effects when St John's wort given with 5HT1 agonists—avoid concomitant use
Amitriptyline St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of amitriptyline
Amlodipine St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of amlodipine
Antidepressants, SSRIincreased serotonergic effects when St John's wort given with SSRIs—avoid concomitant use
Antiepilepticsavoid concomitant use of St John's wort with antiepileptics
Aprepitantavoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of aprepitant
Aripiprazole St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of aripiprazole—increase dose of aripiprazole
Atazanavir St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of atazanavir—avoid concomitant use
Cabazitaxelavoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of cabazitaxel
Ciclosporin St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of ciclosporin—avoid concomitant use
Coumarins St John's wort reduces anticoagulant effect of coumarins (avoid concomitant use)Change in patient’s clinical condition, particularly associated with liver disease, intercurrent illness, or drug administration, necessitates more frequent testing. Major changes in diet (especially involving salads and vegetables) and in alcohol consumption may also affect anticoagulant control
Dabigatran Etexilate St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of dabigatran etexilate—manufacturer of dabigatran etexilate advises avoid concomitant use
Darunavir St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of darunavir—avoid concomitant use
Digoxin St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of digoxin—avoid concomitant use
Dronedarone St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of dronedarone—avoid concomitant use
Duloxetinepossible increased serotonergic effects when St John's wort given with duloxetine
Efavirenz St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of efavirenz—avoid concomitant use
Eplerenone St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of eplerenone—avoid concomitant use
Eribulin St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of eribulin
Etravirineavoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of etravirine
Everolimus St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of everolimus—manufacturer of everolimus advises avoid concomitant use
Fosamprenavir St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of fosamprenavir—avoid concomitant useFosamprenavir is a prodrug of amprenavir
Gefitinibavoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of gefitinib
Imatinib St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of imatinib—avoid concomitant use
Indinavir St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of indinavir—avoid concomitant use
Irinotecan St John's wort accelerates metabolism of irinotecan (reduced plasma concentration—avoid concomitant use)
Ivabradine St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of ivabradine—avoid concomitant use
Lapatinibavoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of lapatinib
Lopinavir St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of lopinavir—avoid concomitant useIn combination with ritonavir as Kaletra® (ritonavir is present to inhibit lopinavir metabolism and increase plasma-lopinavir concentration)—see also Ritonavir
Maraviroc St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of maraviroc—avoid concomitant use
Methadone St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of methadone
Midazolam St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of oral midazolam
Nelfinavir St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of nelfinavir—avoid concomitant use
Nevirapine St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of nevirapine—avoid concomitant use
Nifedipine St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of nifedipine
Oestrogens St John's wort reduces contraceptive effect of oestrogens (avoid concomitant use)Interactions of combined oral contraceptives may also apply to combined contraceptive patches and vaginal rings, see section 7.3.1
Omeprazole St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of omeprazole
Progestogens St John's wort reduces contraceptive effect of progestogens (avoid concomitant use)Interactions of combined oral contraceptives may also apply to combined contraceptive patches and vaginal rings, see section 7.3.1. For further information on interactions of oral progestogen-only contraceptives, see also section 7.3.2.1; parenteral progestogen-only contraceptives, see also section 7.3.2.2; the intra-uterine progestogen-only device, see also section 7.3.2.3; hormonal emergency contraception, see also section 7.3.5
Ritonavir St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of ritonavir—avoid concomitant use
Saquinavir St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of saquinavir—avoid concomitant use
Simvastatin St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of simvastatin
Tacrolimus St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of tacrolimus—avoid concomitant useInteractions do not generally apply to tacrolimus used topically; risk of facial flushing and skin irritation with alcohol consumption (see section 13.5.3) does not apply to tacrolimus taken systemically
Telapreviravoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of telaprevir
Telithromycin St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of telithromycin (avoid during and for 2 weeks after St John's wort)
Theophylline St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of theophylline—avoid concomitant use
Tipranavir St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of tipranavir—avoid concomitant use
Ulipristalavoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of ulipristal (contraceptive effect of ulipristal possibly reduced)
Venlafaxinepossible increased serotonergic effects when St John's wort given with venlafaxine
Verapamil St John's wort significantly reduces plasma concentration of verapamil
Vinflunine St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of vinflunine—manufacturer of vinflunine advises avoid concomitant use
Voriconazole St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of voriconazole—avoid concomitant use

St John's Wort belongs to Antidepressants and will have the following interactions:

Artemether with Lumefantrineavoidance of antidepressants advised by manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine
Atomoxetinepossible increased risk of convulsions when antidepressants given with atomoxetine
