    HYDROCORTISONE

    Indications

     

    mild inflammatory skin disorders such as eczemas (but for over-the-counter preparations, see below); nappy rash (see also section 13.2.2)

    Cautions

     

    see notes above

    Contra-indications

     

    see notes above

    Side-effects

     

    see notes above

    Dose

    • Apply thinly 1–2 times daily

    Sub-sections

    • Hydrocortisone
    • Over-the-counter hydrocortisone preparations
    • Proprietary hydrocortisone preparations
      • Dioderm®
      • Mildison®

    Compound preparations

    • Alphaderm®

    Calmurid HC® (Galderma)

    Cream, hydrocortisone 1%, urea 10%, lactic acid 5%, net price 100 g = £8.76. Label: 28, counselling, application. Potency: moderate

    Excipients none as listed in section 13.1.3.

    Note

     

    If stinging occurs, manufacturer advises dilute to half-strength with aqueous cream for 1 week then transfer to undiluted preparation (but see section 13.1.1 for advice to avoid dilution where possible)
