FLUTICASONE PROPIONATE WITH SALMETEROL

Additional information

 

interactions (Fluticasone,Salmeterol).

Indications

 

see under preparations (see also Management of Chronic Asthma table)

Cautions

 

see section 3.1.1.1 and Cautions of Inhaled Corticosteroids (section 3.2)

Pregnancy

 

see section 3.1

Breast-feeding

 

see section 3.1

Side-effects

 

see section 3.1.1.1 and Side-effects of Inhaled Corticosteroids (section 3.2); also sinusitis, nasopharyngitis, very rarely dyspepsia, arthralgia; also reported nausea, dizziness, rash

Dose

  • See under preparations

Counselling

 

Advise patients that Seretide should not be used for relief of acute attacks, not to exceed prescribed dose, and to follow manufacturer’s directions; if a previously effective dose fails to provide adequate relief, a doctor’s advice should be obtained as soon as possible
