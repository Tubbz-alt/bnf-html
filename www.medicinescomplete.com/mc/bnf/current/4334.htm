ETHINYLESTRADIOL

(Ethinyloestradiol)

Additional information interactions (Ethinylestradiol).

Indications

 

see notes above

Cautions

 

cardiovascular disease (sodium retention with oedema, thromboembolism); see also under Combined Hormonal Contraceptives (section 7.3.1) and under Oestrogens for HRT

Contra-indications

 

see under Combined Hormonal Contraceptives (section 7.3.1) and under Oestrogens for HRT

Hepatic impairment

 

avoid; see also Combined Hormonal Contraceptives, section 7.3.1

Pregnancy

 

see Combined Hormonal Contraceptives, section 7.3.1

Breast-feeding

 

see Combined Hormonal Contraceptives, section 7.3.1

Side-effects

 

feminising effects in men; see also under Combined Hormonal Contraceptives (section 7.3.1) and under Oestrogens for HRT

Dose

 

Menopausal symptoms and osteoporosis prophylaxis, (with progestogen for 12–14 days per cycle in women with intact uterus), 10–50 micrograms daily for 21 days, repeated after 7-day tablet-free period

Female hypogonadism, 10–50 micrograms daily, usually on cyclical basis; initial oestrogen therapy should be followed by combined oestrogen and progestogen therapy

Menstrual disorders, 20–50 micrograms daily from day 5 to 25 of each cycle, with progestogen added either throughout the cycle or from day 15 to 25
