Muscle Relaxants, depolarising

Muscle Relaxants, depolarising has no specific interaction information.

Muscle Relaxants, depolarising belongs to Muscle Relaxants and will have the following interactions:

Lithiumeffects of muscle relaxants enhanced by lithium
Propranololeffects of muscle relaxants enhanced by propranolol

Suxamethonium belongs to Muscle Relaxants, depolarising and has the following interaction information:

Aminoglycosideseffects of suxamethonium enhanced by aminoglycosides
Anaesthetics, General (volatile liquids)effects of suxamethonium enhanced by volatile liquid general anaesthetics
Bambuteroleffects of suxamethonium enhanced by bambuterol
Cardiac Glycosidesrisk of ventricular arrhythmias when suxamethonium given with cardiac glycosides
Clindamycineffects of suxamethonium enhanced by clindamycin
Cyclophosphamideeffects of suxamethonium enhanced by cyclophosphamide
Donepezileffects of suxamethonium possibly enhanced by donepezil
Edrophoniumeffects of suxamethonium enhanced by edrophonium
Galantamineeffects of suxamethonium enhanced by galantamine
Lidocaineneuromuscular blockade enhanced and prolonged when suxamethonium given with lidocaine Interactions less likely when lidocaine used topically
Magnesium (parenteral)effects of suxamethonium enhanced by parenteral magnesium
Metoclopramideeffects of suxamethonium enhanced by metoclopramide
Neostigmineeffects of suxamethonium enhanced by neostigmine
Phenelzineeffects of suxamethonium enhanced by phenelzine
Piperacillineffects of suxamethonium enhanced by piperacillin
Polymyxinseffects of suxamethonium enhanced by polymyxins
Promazineeffects of suxamethonium possibly enhanced by promazine
Propofolincreased risk of myocardial depression and bradycardia when suxamethonium given with propofol
Pyridostigmineeffects of suxamethonium enhanced by pyridostigmine
Quinineeffects of suxamethonium possibly enhanced by quinine
Rivastigmineeffects of suxamethonium enhanced by rivastigmine
Thiotepaeffects of suxamethonium enhanced by thiotepa
Vancomycineffects of suxamethonium enhanced by vancomycin
Verapamileffects of suxamethonium enhanced by verapamil
