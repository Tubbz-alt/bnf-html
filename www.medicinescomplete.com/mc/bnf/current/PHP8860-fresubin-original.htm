Fresubin® Original (Fresenius Kabi)

Not suitable for use in child under 1 year; not recommended for child 1–6 years

Liquid (sip or tube feed) per 100 mL

Energy 420 kJ (100 kcal)

Protein 3.8 g, cows' milk, soya

Carbohydrate 13.8 g, (sugars 3.5 g(1))

Fat 3.4 g

Fibre Nil

Special characteristics Gluten-free. Residual lactose. Contains fish gelatin. Feed in flexible pack contains fish oil and fish gelatin

ACBS Indications Standard

Presentation and flavour Bottle: 200 mL = £1.85. Black currant, chocolate, nut, peach, vanilla. Flexible pack: 500 mL = £3.59, 1000 mL = £7.10, 1500 mL = £10.66
