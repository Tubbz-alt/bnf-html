HYDROMORPHONE HYDROCHLORIDE

Additional information

 

interactions (Hydromorphone).

Indications

 

severe pain in cancer

Cautions

 

see notes above; also pancreatitis; toxic psychosis

Contra-indications

 

see notes above; also acute abdomen

Hepatic impairment

 

see notes above

Renal impairment

 

see notes above

Pregnancy

 

see notes above

Breast-feeding

 

avoid—no information available

Side-effects

 

see notes above; also paralytic ileus, peripheral oedema, seizures, asthenia, dyskinesia, agitation, and tremor

Dose

  • See under preparations below

Sub-sections

  • Palladone®

Modified release

Palladone® SR (Napp)

Capsules, m/r, hydromorphone hydrochloride 2 mg (yellow/clear), net price 56-cap pack = £20.98; 4 mg (pale blue/clear), 56-cap pack = £28.75; 8 mg (pink/clear), 56-cap pack = £56.08; 16 mg (brown/clear), 56-cap pack = £106.53; 24 mg (dark blue/clear), 56-cap pack = £159.82. Label: 2, counselling, see below

Dose 4 mg every 12 hours, increased if necessary according to severity of pain; child under 12 years not recommended

Counselling

 

Swallow whole or open capsule and sprinkle contents on soft food
