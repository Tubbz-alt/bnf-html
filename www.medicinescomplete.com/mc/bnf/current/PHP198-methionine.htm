METHIONINE

Indications

 

paracetamol overdosage, see notes above

Hepatic impairment

 

may precipitate coma

Side-effects

 

nausea, vomiting, drowsiness, irritability

Dose

  • adult and child over 6 years initially 2.5 g, followed by 3 further doses of 2.5 g every 4 hours, child under 6 years initially 1 g, followed by 3 further doses of 1 g every 4 hours
