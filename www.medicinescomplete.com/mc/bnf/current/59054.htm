ADAPALENE

Indications

 

mild to moderate acne

Cautions

 

see notes above

Pregnancy

 

see notes above

Breast-feeding

 

amount of drug in milk probably too small to be harmful; ensure infant does not come in contact with treated areas

Side-effects

 

see notes above

Dose

 

adult and child over 12 years, apply thinly once daily in the evening
