MAGNESIUM SALTS

Additional information

 

interactions (Magnesium Salts (oral)).

Indications

 

see under preparations below

Cautions

 

elderly and debilitated; see also notes above; interactions: Appendix 1 (antacids)

Contra-indications

 

acute gastro-intestinal conditions

Hepatic impairment

 

avoid in hepatic coma if risk of renal failure

Renal impairment

 

avoid or reduce dose; increased risk of toxicity

Side-effects

 

colic
