    St John's Wort

    St John's Wort has the following interaction information:

    5HT1-receptor Agonists

    increased serotonergic effects when St John's wort given with 5HT1 agonists—avoid concomitant use

    Amitriptyline

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of amitriptyline

    Amlodipine

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of amlodipine

    Antidepressants, SSRI

    increased serotonergic effects when St John's wort given with SSRIs—avoid concomitant use

    Antiepileptics

    avoid concomitant use of St John's wort with antiepileptics

    Aprepitant

    avoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of aprepitant

    Aripiprazole

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of aripiprazole—increase dose of aripiprazole

    Atazanavir

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of atazanavir—avoid concomitant use

    Axitinib

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of axitinib—consider increasing dose of axitinib

    Cabazitaxel

    avoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of cabazitaxel

    Ciclosporin

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of ciclosporin—avoid concomitant use

    Coumarins

    St John's wort reduces anticoagulant effect of coumarins (avoid concomitant use)

    Note: Change in patient's clinical condition, particularly associated with liver disease, intercurrent illness, or drug administration, necessitates more frequent testing. Major changes in diet (especially involving salads and vegetables) and in alcohol consumption may also affect anticoagulant control

    Crizotinib

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of crizotinib—manufacturer of crizotinib advises avoid concomitant use

    Dabigatran Etexilate

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of dabigatran etexilate—manufacturer of dabigatran etexilate advises avoid concomitant use

    Darunavir

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of darunavir—avoid concomitant use

    Digoxin

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of digoxin—avoid concomitant use

    Dronedarone

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of dronedarone—avoid concomitant use

    Duloxetine

    possible increased serotonergic effects when St John's wort given with duloxetine

    Efavirenz

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of efavirenz—avoid concomitant use

    Eplerenone

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of eplerenone—avoid concomitant use

    Eribulin

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of eribulin

    Etravirine

    avoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of etravirine

    Everolimus

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of everolimus—manufacturer of everolimus advises avoid concomitant use

    Fosamprenavir

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of fosamprenavir—avoid concomitant use

    Note: Fosamprenavir is a prodrug of amprenavir

    Gefitinib

    avoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of gefitinib

    Imatinib

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of imatinib—avoid concomitant use

    Indinavir

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of indinavir—avoid concomitant use

    Irinotecan

    St John's wort accelerates metabolism of irinotecan (reduced plasma concentration—avoid concomitant use)

    Ivabradine

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of ivabradine—avoid concomitant use

    Lapatinib

    avoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of lapatinib

    Lopinavir

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of lopinavir—avoid concomitant use

    Note: In combination with ritonavir as Kaletra® (ritonavir is present to inhibit lopinavir metabolism and increase plasma-lopinavir concentration)—see also Ritonavir

    Maraviroc

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of maraviroc—avoid concomitant use

    Methadone

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of methadone

    Midazolam

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of oral midazolam

    Nevirapine

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of nevirapine—avoid concomitant use

    Nifedipine

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of nifedipine

    Oestrogens

    St John's wort reduces contraceptive effect of oestrogens (avoid concomitant use)

    Note: Interactions of combined oral contraceptives may also apply to combined contraceptive patches and vaginal rings, see section 7.3.1

    Omeprazole

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of omeprazole

    Progestogens

    St John's wort reduces contraceptive effect of progestogens (avoid concomitant use)

    Note: Interactions of combined oral contraceptives may also apply to combined contraceptive patches and vaginal rings, see section 7.3.1. For further information on interactions of oral progestogen-only contraceptives, see also section 7.3.2.1; parenteral progestogen-only contraceptives, see also section 7.3.2.2; the intra-uterine progestogen-only device, see also section 7.3.2.3; hormonal emergency contraception, see also section 7.3.5

    Rilpivirine

    avoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of rilpivirine (plasma concentration of rilpivirine possibly reduced)

    Ritonavir

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of ritonavir—avoid concomitant use

    Saquinavir

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of saquinavir—avoid concomitant use

    Simvastatin

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of simvastatin

    Tacrolimus

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of tacrolimus—avoid concomitant use

    Note: Interactions do not generally apply to tacrolimus used topically; risk of facial flushing and skin irritation with alcohol consumption (see section 13.5.3) does not apply to tacrolimus taken systemically

    Telaprevir

    avoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of telaprevir

    Telithromycin

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of telithromycin (avoid during and for 2 weeks after St John's wort)

    Theophylline

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of theophylline—avoid concomitant use

    Tipranavir

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of tipranavir—avoid concomitant use

    Ulipristal

    avoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of ulipristal (contraceptive effect of ulipristal possibly reduced)

    Vandetanib

    avoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of vandetanib (plasma concentration of vandetanib possibly reduced)

    Vemurafenib

    avoidance of St John's wort advised by manufacturer of vemurafenib

    Venlafaxine

    possible increased serotonergic effects when St John's wort given with venlafaxine

    Verapamil

    St John's wort significantly reduces plasma concentration of verapamil

    Vinflunine

    St John's wort possibly reduces plasma concentration of vinflunine—manufacturer of vinflunine advises avoid concomitant use

    Voriconazole

    St John's wort reduces plasma concentration of voriconazole—avoid concomitant use

    St John's Wort belongs to Antidepressants and will have the following interaction information:

    Artemether with Lumefantrine

    avoidance of antidepressants advised by manufacturer of artemether with lumefantrine

    Atomoxetine

    possible increased risk of convulsions when antidepressants given with atomoxetine

    Piperaquine with Artenimol

    avoidance of antidepressants advised by manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol

    Note: Piperaquine has a long half-life; there is a potential for drug interactions to occur for up to 3 months after treatment has been stopped
