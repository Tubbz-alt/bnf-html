Cytotoxics

Cytotoxics has the following interaction information:

Clozapine

avoid concomitant use of cytotoxics with clozapine (increased risk of agranulocytosis)

Note: Avoid concomitant use of clozapine with drugs that have a substantial potential for causing agranulocytosis

St John's Wort

plasma concentration of eribulin possibly reduced by St John's wort

Arsenic Trioxide belongs to Cytotoxics and has the following interaction information:

Acetazolamide

risk of ventricular arrhythmias with arsenic trioxide increased by hypokalaemia caused by acetazolamide

Amiodarone

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with amiodarone

Note: Amiodarone has a long half-life; there is a potential for drug interactions to occur for several weeks (or even months) after treatment with it has been stopped

Amitriptyline

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with amitriptyline

Amphotericin

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with amphotericin

Note: Close monitoring required with concomitant administration of nephrotoxic drugs or cytotoxics

Antipsychotics

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with antipsychotics that prolong the QT interval

Note: Increased risk of toxicity with myelosuppressive drugs

Clomipramine

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with clomipramine

Disopyramide

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with disopyramide

Diuretics, Loop

risk of ventricular arrhythmias with arsenic trioxide increased by hypokalaemia caused by loop diuretics

Diuretics, Thiazide and related

risk of ventricular arrhythmias with arsenic trioxide increased by hypokalaemia caused by thiazides and related diuretics

Erythromycin

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with erythromycin

Note: Interactions do not apply to small amounts of erythromycin used topically

Haloperidol

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with haloperidol

Levofloxacin

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with levofloxacin

Lithium

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with lithium

Moxifloxacin

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with moxifloxacin

Sotalol

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with sotalol

Vandetanib

possible increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when arsenic trioxide given with vandetanib —avoid concomitant use
