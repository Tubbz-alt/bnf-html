    OFLOXACIN

    Indications

     

    see notes above

    Cautions

     

    corneal ulcer or epithelial defect (risk of corneal perforation)

    Pregnancy

     

    manufacturer advises use only if benefit outweighs risk; systemic quinolones have caused arthropathy in animal studies

    Breast-feeding

     

    manufacturer advises avoid

    Side-effects

     

    ocular discomfort and irritation; also reported facial oedema, keratitis, visual disturbances, photophobia, increased lacrimation, ocular oedema, dry eyes, ocular hyperaemia

    Dose

    • adult and child over 1 month, apply every 2–4 hours for the first 2 days, then reduce frequency to 4 times daily (max. 10 days treatment)
