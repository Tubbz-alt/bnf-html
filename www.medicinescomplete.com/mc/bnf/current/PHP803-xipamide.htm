XIPAMIDE

Additional information

 

interactions (Xipamide).

Indications

 

oedema, hypertension (see also notes above)

Cautions

 

see notes above; also acute porphyria (section 9.8.2)

Contra-indications

 

see notes above

Hepatic impairment

 

see notes above

Renal impairment

 

see notes above

Pregnancy

 

see notes above

Breast-feeding

 

no information available

Side-effects

 

see notes above

Dose

  • Oedema, initially 40 mg daily in the morning, increased to 80 mg in resistant cases; maintenance 20 mg in the morning

  • Hypertension, 20 mg daily in the morning
