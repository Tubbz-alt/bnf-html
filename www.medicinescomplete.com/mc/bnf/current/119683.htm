VARICELLA-ZOSTER VACCINE

Additional information interactions (Varicella-zoster Vaccine).

Indications

 

immunisation against varicella infection (see notes above)

Cautions

 

see section 14.1; also post-vaccination close-contact with susceptible individuals (see notes above); interactions: Appendix 1 (vaccines)

Contra-indications

 

see section 14.1

Pregnancy

 

avoid pregnancy for 3 months after vaccination; see also section 14.1

Breast-feeding

 

see section 14.1

Side-effects

 

see section 14.1; also varicella-like rash; rarely thrombocytopenia

Dose

 

See under preparations

Sub-sections

  • Varilrix® (GSK)

    Injection, powder for reconstitution, live attenuated varicella–zoster virus (Oka strain) propagated in human diploid cells, net price 0.5-mL vial (with diluent) = £27.31
    Excipients include neomycin

    Dose

     

    by subcutaneous injection preferably into deltoid region, adult and child over 1 year (see notes above), 2 doses of 0.5 mL separated by an interval of at least 6 weeks (minimum 4 weeks)
