Resource® Dessert Energy (Nestlé)

Not suitable for use in child under 1 year; use with caution in child 1–5 years

Semi-solid per 100 g

Energy 671 kJ (160 kcal)

Protein 4.8 g, cows' milk

Carbohydrate 21.2 g, (sugars 9.9 g(1))

Fat 6.2 g

Fibre Nil

Special characteristics Gluten-free. Contains lactose

ACBS Indications Standard; also CAPD, haemodialysis

Presentation and flavour Cup: 125 g = £1.54. Caramel, chocolate, vanilla
