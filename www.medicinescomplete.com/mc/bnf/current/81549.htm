CLOTRIMAZOLE

Indications

 

fungal skin infections; vaginal candidiasis (section 7.2.2); otitis externa (section 12.1.1)

Cautions

 

see notes above

Pregnancy

 

minimal absorption from skin; not known to be harmful

Side-effects

 

see notes above

Dose

 

Apply 2–3 times daily

Sub-sections

  • Clotrimazole (Non-proprietary)

    Cream, clotrimazole 1%, net price 20 g = £1.40
