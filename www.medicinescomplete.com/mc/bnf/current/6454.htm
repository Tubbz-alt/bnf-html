14.4 Vaccines and antisera

Availability

 

Anthrax and yellow fever vaccines, botulism antitoxin, diphtheria antitoxin, and snake and spider venom antitoxins are available from local designated holding centres.

For antivenom, see Emergency Treatment of Poisoning, Snake Bites and Animal Stings.

Enquiries for vaccines not available commercially can also be made to:

Immunisation Policy, Monitoring and Surveillance
Department of Health
Wellington House
133–155 Waterloo Road
London
SE1 8UG
Email: vaccine.supply@dh.gsi.gov.uk

In Scotland information about availability of vaccines can be obtained from a Specialist in Pharmaceutical Public Health. In Wales enquiries for vaccines not available commercially should be directed to:

Welsh Medicines Information Centre
University Hospital of Wales
Cardiff
CF14 4XW
Tel: (029) 2074 2979

and in Northern Ireland:

Pharmacy and Medicines Management Centre
Beech House
Antrim Hospital Site
Northern Health and Social Care Trust
Bush Road
Antrim
BT41 2RL
Email: rphps.admin@northerntrust.hscni.net

For further details of availability, see under individual vaccines.
