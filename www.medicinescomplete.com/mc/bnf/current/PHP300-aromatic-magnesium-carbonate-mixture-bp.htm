MAGNESIUM CARBONATE

Additional information

 

interactions (Magnesium Salts (oral)).

Indications

 

dyspepsia

Cautions

 

see notes above; interactions: Appendix 1 (antacids)

Contra-indications

 

hypophosphataemia

Hepatic impairment

 

see notes above

Renal impairment

 

see notes above; magnesium carbonate mixture has a high sodium content

Side-effects

 

diarrhoea; belching due to liberated carbon dioxide

Sub-sections

Aromatic Magnesium Carbonate Mixture, BP(Aromatic Magnesium Carbonate Oral Suspension)

Oral suspension, light magnesium carbonate 3%, sodium bicarbonate 5%, in a suitable vehicle containing aromatic cardamom tincture. Contains about 6 mmol Na+/10 mL. Net price 200 mL = 66p

Dose 10 mL 3 times daily in water

For preparations also containing aluminium, see above and section 1.1.2.
