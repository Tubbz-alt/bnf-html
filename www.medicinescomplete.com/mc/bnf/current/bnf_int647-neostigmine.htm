    Neostigmine

    Neostigmine has the following interaction information:

    Aminoglycosides

    effects of neostigmine antagonised by aminoglycosides

    Chloroquine and Hydroxychloroquine

    effects of neostigmine may be diminished because of potential for chloroquine and hydroxychloroquine to increase symptoms of myasthenia gravis

    Clindamycin

    effects of neostigmine antagonised by clindamycin

    Lithium

    effects of neostigmine antagonised by lithium

    Muscle Relaxants, non-depolarising

    neostigmine antagonises effects of non-depolarising muscle relaxants

    Polymyxins

    effects of neostigmine antagonised by polymyxins

    Propafenone

    effects of neostigmine possibly antagonised by propafenone

    Propranolol

    effects of neostigmine antagonised by propranolol

    Suxamethonium

    neostigmine enhances effects of suxamethonium

    Neostigmine belongs to Parasympathomimetics and will have the following interaction information:

    Antimuscarinics

    effects of parasympathomimetics antagonised by antimuscarinics

    Note: Many drugs have antimuscarinic effects; concomitant use of two or more such drugs can increase side-effects such as dry mouth, urine retention, and constipation; concomitant use can also lead to confusion in the elderly. Interactions do not generally apply to antimuscarinics used by inhalation
