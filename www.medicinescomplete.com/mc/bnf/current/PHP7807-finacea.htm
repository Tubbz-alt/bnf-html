    AZELAIC ACID

    Indications

     

    see preparations

    Cautions

     

    avoid contact with eyes, mouth, and mucous membranes

    Side-effects

     

    local irritation (reduce frequency or discontinue temporarily); less commonly skin discoloration; also reported worsening of asthma

    Sub-sections

    Finacea® (Bayer)

    Gel, azelaic acid 15%, net price 30 g = £7.48

    Excipients include disodium edetate, polysorbate 80, propylene glycol

    Dose facial acne vulgaris, adult and child over 12 years, apply twice daily; discontinue if no improvement after 1 month

    Papulopustular rosacea, adult over 18 years, apply twice daily; discontinue if no improvement after 2 months
