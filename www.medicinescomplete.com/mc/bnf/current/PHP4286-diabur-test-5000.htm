Urinalysis

Reagent strips are available for measuring for glucose in the urine. Tests for ketones by patients are rarely required unless they become unwell—see also Blood Monitoring.

Microalbuminuria can be detected with Micral-Test II® but this should be followed by confirmation in the laboratory, since false positive results are common.
