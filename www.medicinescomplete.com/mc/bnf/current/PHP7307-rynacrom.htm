SODIUM CROMOGLICATE(Sodium Cromoglycate)

Indications

 

prophylaxis of allergic rhinitis

Side-effects

 

local irritation; rarely transient bronchospasm

Sub-sections

Rynacrom® (Sanofi-Aventis)

4% aqueous nasal spray, sodium cromoglicate 4% (5.2 mg/spray). Net price 22 mL (150-spray unit with pump) = £17.07

Excipients include benzalkonium chloride, disodium edetate

Dose adult and child, 1 spray into each nostril 2–4 times daily
