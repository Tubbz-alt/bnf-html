Local treatment

Sub-sections

AS Saliva Orthana® (AS Pharma)

Oral spray, gastric mucin (porcine) 3.5%, xylitol 2%, sodium fluoride 4.2 mg/litre, with preservatives and flavouring agents, pH neutral. Net price 50-mL bottle = £4.92; 500-mL refill = £34.27

Dose ACBS: patients suffering from dry mouth as a result of having (or having undergone) radiotherapy, or sicca syndrome, spray 2–3 times onto oral and pharyngeal mucosa, when required

Dental prescribing on NHS

 

AS Saliva Orthana® Oral Spray may be prescribed

Lozenges, mucin 65 mg, xylitol 59 mg, in a sorbitol basis, pH neutral. Net price 30-lozenge pack = £3.50

Dose ACBS: patients suffering from dry mouth as a result of having (or having undergone) radiotherapy, or sicca syndrome, allow 1 lozenge to dissolve slowly in the mouth when required

Note

 

AS Saliva Orthana® lozenges do not contain fluoride

Dental prescribing on NHS

 

AS Saliva Orthana® Lozenges may be prescribed
