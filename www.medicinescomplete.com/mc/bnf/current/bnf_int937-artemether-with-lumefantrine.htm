Artemether with Lumefantrine

Artemether with Lumefantrine has the following interaction information:

Amiodarone

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with amiodarone (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

Note: Amiodarone has a long half-life; there is a potential for drug interactions to occur for several weeks (or even months) after treatment with it has been stopped

Antidepressants

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with antidepressants

Antifungals, Imidazole

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with imidazoles

Antifungals, Triazole

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with triazoles

Antimalarials

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with antimalarials

Antipsychotics

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with antipsychotics

Note: Increased risk of toxicity with myelosuppressive drugs

Atazanavir

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises caution with atazanavir

Boceprevir

avoidance of artemether/lumefantrine advised by manufacturer of boceprevir

Cimetidine

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with cimetidine

Darunavir

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises caution with darunavir

Disopyramide

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with disopyramide (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

Flecainide

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with flecainide (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

Fosamprenavir

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises caution with fosamprenavir

Note: Fosamprenavir is a prodrug of amprenavir

Grapefruit Juice

plasma concentration of artemether/lumefantrine possibly increased by grapefruit juice

Indinavir

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises caution with indinavir

Lopinavir

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises caution with lopinavir

Note: In combination with ritonavir as Kaletra® (ritonavir is present to inhibit lopinavir metabolism and increase plasma-lopinavir concentration)—see also Ritonavir

Macrolides

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with macrolides

Note: See also Telithromycin

Metoprolol

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with metoprolol

Nelfinavir

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises caution with nelfinavir

Quinine

increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when artemether/lumefantrine given with quinine

Quinolones

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with quinolones

Ritonavir

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises caution with ritonavir

Saquinavir

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises caution with saquinavir

Sotalol

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises avoid concomitant use with sotalol

Tipranavir

manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine advises caution with tipranavir

Vandetanib

possible increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when artemether/lumefantrine given with vandetanib —avoid concomitant use

Artemether with Lumefantrine belongs to Antimalarials and will have the following interaction information:

Artemether with Lumefantrine

avoidance of antimalarials advised by manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine

Histamine

avoidance of antimalarials advised by manufacturer of histamine

Typhoid Vaccine (oral)

antimalarials inactivate oral typhoid vaccine —see under Typhoid vaccines, section 14.4

Lumefantrine belongs to Artemether with Lumefantrine but has no specific interaction information.

Note: Lumefantrine interactions as for artemether with lumefantrine
