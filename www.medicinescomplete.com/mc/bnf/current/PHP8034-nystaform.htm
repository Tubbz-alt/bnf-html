NYSTATIN

Indications

 

skin infections due to Candida spp.; oral fungal infections (section 12.3.2)

Cautions

 

see notes above

Side-effects

 

see notes above

Sub-sections

Nystaform® (Typharm)

Cream, nystatin 100 000 units/g, chlorhexidine hydrochloride 1%, net price 30 g = £2.62

Excipients include benzyl alcohol, cetostearyl alcohol, polysorbate 60

Dose apply 2–3 times daily continuing for 7 days after lesions have healed.
