Lansoprazole

Lansoprazole has the following interaction information:

Antacids

absorption of lansoprazole possibly reduced by antacids

Note: Antacids should preferably not be taken at the same time as other drugs since they may impair absorption

Clopidogrel

lansoprazole possibly reduces antiplatelet effect of clopidogrel

Erlotinib

avoidance of lansoprazole advised by manufacturer of erlotinib

Fluvoxamine

plasma concentration of lansoprazole possibly increased by fluvoxamine

Rilpivirine

avoidance of lansoprazole advised by manufacturer of rilpivirine (plasma concentration of rilpivirine possibly reduced)

Saquinavir

lansoprazole possibly increases plasma concentration of saquinavir —manufacturer of saquinavir advises avoid concomitant use

Sucralfate

absorption of lansoprazole possibly reduced by sucralfate

Lansoprazole belongs to Proton Pump Inhibitors and will have the following interaction information:

Atazanavir

proton pump inhibitors reduce plasma concentration of atazanavir —avoid or adjust dose of both drugs (consult product literature)

Digoxin

proton pump inhibitors possibly slightly increase plasma concentration of digoxin

Itraconazole

proton pump inhibitors reduce absorption of itraconazole

Ketoconazole

proton pump inhibitors reduce absorption of ketoconazole

Lapatinib

proton pump inhibitors possibly reduce absorption of lapatinib

Methotrexate

proton pump inhibitors possibly reduce excretion of methotrexate (increased risk of toxicity)

Posaconazole

avoidance of proton pump inhibitors advised by manufacturer of posaconazole (plasma concentration of posaconazole possibly reduced)

Raltegravir

proton pump inhibitors possibly increase plasma concentration of raltegravir —manufacturer of raltegravir advises avoid concomitant use

Ulipristal

avoidance of proton pump inhibitors advised by manufacturer of ulipristal (plasma concentration of ulipristal possibly reduced)

Vandetanib

avoidance of proton pump inhibitors advised by manufacturer of vandetanib (absorption of vandetanib possibly reduced)

Lansoprazole belongs to Ulcer-healing Drugs but Ulcer-healing Drugs has no interactions information.
