    Not suitable for use in child under 1 year; use with caution in child 1–5 years

    Liquid (sip or tube feed) per 100 mL

    Energy 632 kJ, (150 kcal)(1)

    Protein 6.25 g, cows’ milk, soy protein isolate

    Carbohydrate 20.2 g, (sugars 1.13 g)

    Fat 4.92 g

    Fibre Nil

    Special characteristics Gluten-free. Residual lactose

    ACBS Indications Standard; also CAPD, haemodialysis

    Presentation and flavour Bottle: 220 mL = £1.98. Chicken, mushroom
