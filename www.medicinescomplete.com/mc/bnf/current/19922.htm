ANTIBACTERIALS

Indications

 

acne vulgaris

Cautions

 

some manufacturers advise preparations containing alcohol are not suitable for use with benzoyl peroxide

Sub-sections

  • Dalacin T®

  • Stiemycin® (Stiefel)

    Solution, erythromycin 2% in an alcoholic basis, net price 50 mL = £7.69
    Excipients include propylene glycol

    Dose

     

    adult and child over 12 years, apply thinly twice daily

  • Zindaclin®
  • Zineryt®
