OLMESARTAN MEDOXOMIL

Additional information

 

interactions (Olmesartan).

Indications

 

hypertension (see also notes above)

Cautions

 

see notes above

Contra-indications

 

biliary obstruction

Hepatic impairment

 

dose should not exceed 20 mg daily in moderate impairment; manufacturer advises avoid in severe impairment—no information available

Renal impairment

 

max. 20 mg daily if eGFR 20–60 mL/minute/1.73 m2; avoid if eGFR less than 20 mL/minute/1.73 m2

Pregnancy

 

see notes above

Breast-feeding

 

see notes above

Side-effects

 

see notes above; also gastro-intestinal disturbances; chest pain, peripheral oedema, hypertriglyceridaemia; fatigue; influenza-like symptoms, cough, pharyngitis, rhinitis; urinary-tract infection; haematuria, hyperuricaemia; arthritis, musculoskeletal pain; less commonly angina, vertigo, rash; very rarely headache, thrombocytopenia, myalgia, pruritus, urticaria

Dose

  • Initially 10 mg once daily; if necessary increased to 20 mg once daily; max. 40 mg daily
