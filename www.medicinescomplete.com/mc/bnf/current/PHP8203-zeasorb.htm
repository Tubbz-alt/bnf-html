    ALUMINIUM SALTS

    Indications

     

    see under Dose below

    Cautions

     

    avoid contact with eyes or mucous membranes; avoid use on broken or irritated skin; do not shave axillae or use depilatories within 12 hours of application; avoid contact with clothing

    Side-effects

     

    skin irritation

    Dose

    • Hyperhidrosis affecting axillae, hands or feet, apply liquid formulation at night to dry skin, wash off the following morning, initially daily then reduce frequency as condition improves—do not bathe immediately before use

    • Hyperhidrosis, bromidrosis, intertrigo, and prevention of tinea pedis and related conditions, apply powder to dry skin

    Sub-sections

    • Anhydrol® Forte
    • Driclor®

    ZeaSORB® (Stiefel)

    Dusting powder, aldioxa 0.22%, chloroxylenol 0.5%, net price 50 g = £2.61

    Excipients include fragrance
