Typhoid Vaccine (oral)

Typhoid Vaccine (oral) has the following interaction information:

Antibacterials

oral typhoid vaccine inactivated by antibacterials —see under Typhoid vaccines, section 14.4

Antimalarials

oral typhoid vaccine inactivated by antimalarials —see under Typhoid vaccines, section 14.4

Typhoid Vaccine (oral) belongs to Vaccines and will have the following interaction information:

Note: For a general warning on live vaccines and high doses of corticosteroids or other immunosuppressive drugs, see section 14.1; for advice on live vaccines and immunoglobulins, see under Normal Immunoglobulin, section 14.5.1

Abatacept

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with abatacept (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)

Adalimumab

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with adalimumab (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)

Anakinra

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with anakinra (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)

Belimumab

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with belimumab (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)

Certolizumab pegol

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with certolizumab pegol (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)

Corticosteroids

immune response to vaccines impaired by high doses of corticosteroids , avoid concomitant use with live vaccines (see under Active immunity, section 14.1)

Note: Interactions do not generally apply to corticosteroids used for topical action (including inhalation) unless specified

Etanercept

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with etanercept (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)

Golimumab

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with golimumab (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)

Infliximab

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with infliximab (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)

Interferon Gamma

avoidance of vaccines advised by manufacturer of interferon gamma

Leflunomide

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with leflunomide (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)

Note: Increased risk of toxicity with other haematotoxic and hepatotoxic drugs

Tocilizumab

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with tocilizumab (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)

Ustekinumab

avoid concomitant use of live vaccines with ustekinumab (see under Active Immunity, section 14.1)
