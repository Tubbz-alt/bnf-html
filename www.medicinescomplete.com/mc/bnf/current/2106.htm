PEPPERMINT OIL

Indications

 

relief of abdominal colic and distension, particularly in irritable bowel syndrome

Cautions

 

sensitivity to menthol

Pregnancy

 

not known to be harmful

Breast-feeding

 

significant levels of menthol in breast milk unlikely

Side-effects

 

heartburn, perianal irritation; rarely, allergic reactions (including rash, headache, bradycardia, muscle tremor, ataxia)

Local irritation

 

Capsules should not be broken or chewed because peppermint oil may irritate mouth or oesophagus

Dose

 

See preparations

  • Mintec® (Almirall)
