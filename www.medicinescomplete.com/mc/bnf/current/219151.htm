Apixaban

Apixaban, a direct inhibitor of activated factor X (factor Xa), is given orally for the prophylaxis of venous thromboembolism in adults after hip or knee replacement surgery; it does not require therapeutic monitoring. Haemorrhage is a common side-effect and patients should be monitored for signs of bleeding or anaemia; treatment should be stopped if severe bleeding occurs.
