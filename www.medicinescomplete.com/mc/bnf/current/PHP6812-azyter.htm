    AZITHROMYCIN DIHYDRATE

    Indications

     

    see notes above

    Side-effects

     

    ocular discomfort (including pruritus, burning), blurred vision; very rarely eyelid ezcema, eyelid erythema, eyelid oedema, conjunctival hyperaemia, keratitis

    Dose

    • Purulent bacterial conjunctivitis, adult and child over 2 years, apply twice daily for 3 days; review if no improvement after 3 days

    • Trachomatous conjunctivitis, adult and child over 1 year, apply twice daily for 3 days; review if no improvement after 3 days

    Sub-sections

    Single use

    Azyter® (Spectrum Thea)

    Eye drops, azithromycin dihydrate 1.5%, net price 6 × 0.25-g = £6.99
