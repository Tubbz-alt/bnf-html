TRIAMCINOLONE

Additional information interactions (Triamcinolone).

Indications

 

suppression of inflammatory and allergic disorders; see also notes above; rheumatic disease (section 10.1.2); skin (section 13.4)

Cautions

 

see notes above; also high dosage may cause proximal myopathy, avoid in chronic therapy

Contra-indications

 

see notes above

Hepatic impairment

 

see notes above

Renal impairment

 

see notes above

Pregnancy

 

see notes above

Breast-feeding

 

see notes above

Side-effects

 

see notes above

Dose

 

By deep intramuscular injection, into gluteal muscle, 40 mg of acetonide for depot effect, repeated at intervals according to the patient’s response; max. single dose 100 mg
