PHENOBARBITAL SODIUM(Phenobarbitone sodium)

Additional information

 

interactions (Phenobarbital).

Indications

 

status epilepticus; other forms of epilepsy except absence seizures (section 4.8.1)

Cautions

 

see Phenobarbital, section 4.8.1; interactions: see Interactions in section 4.8.1 and Appendix 1 (phenobarbital)

Hepatic impairment

 

see Phenobarbital, section 4.8.1

Renal impairment

 

see Phenobarbital, section 4.8.1

Pregnancy

 

see Pregnancy

Breast-feeding

 

see Breast-feeding

Side-effects

 

see Phenobarbital, section 4.8.1

Dose

  • Status epilepticus, by intravenous injection (dilute injection 1 in 10 with water for injections), 10 mg/kg at a rate of not more than 100 mg/minute; max. 1 g

Note

 

For therapeutic purposes phenobarbital and phenobarbital sodium may be considered equivalent in effect

Sub-sections

Phenobarbital (Non-proprietary)

Injection, phenobarbital sodium 15 mg/mL, net price 1-mL amp = £1.64; 30 mg/mL, 1-mL amp = £2.04; 60 mg/mL, 1-mL amp = £2.14; 200 mg/mL, 1-mL amp = £2.00

Excipients include propylene glycol 90% (see Excipients)

Note

 

Must be diluted before intravenous administration (see under Dose)
