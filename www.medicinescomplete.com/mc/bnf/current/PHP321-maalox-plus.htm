Sub-sections

  • Altacite Plus®
  • Asilone®

Maalox Plus® (Sanofi-Aventis)

Suspension, sugar-free, dried aluminium hydroxide 220 mg, simeticone 25 mg, magnesium hydroxide 195 mg/5 mL (low Na+). Net price 500 mL = £2.79

Dose 5–10 mL 4 times daily (after meals and at bedtime) or when required; child under 12 years see BNF for Children
