STERCULIA

Indications

 

see notes above

Cautions

 

see under Ispaghula Husk

Contra-indications

 

see under Ispaghula Husk

Side-effects

 

see under Ispaghula Husk

Dose

 

See under preparations below

Counselling

 

Preparations that swell in contact with liquid should always be carefully swallowed with water and should not be taken immediately before going to bed

Sub-sections

  • Normacol®

  • Normacol Plus® (Norgine)

    Granules, brown, coated, gluten-free, sterculia 62%, frangula (standardised) 8%. Net price 500 g = £6.34; 60 × 7 g sachets = £5.34. Label: 25, 27, counselling, see above

    Dose

     

    constipation and after haemorrhoidectomy, 1–2 heaped 5-mL spoonfuls or the contents of 1–2 sachets washed down without chewing with plenty of liquid once or twice daily after meals; child 6–12 years see BNF for Children
