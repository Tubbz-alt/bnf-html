BETAMETHASONE ESTERS

Indications

 

severe inflammatory skin disorders such as eczemas unresponsive to less potent corticosteroids; psoriasis, see notes above

Cautions

 

see notes above; use of more than 100 g per week of 0.1% preparation likely to cause adrenal suppression

Contra-indications

 

see notes above

Side-effects

 

see notes above

Dose

 

Apply thinly 1–2 times daily
