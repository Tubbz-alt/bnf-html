1.4.1 Adsorbents and bulk-forming drugs

Adsorbents such as kaolin are not recommended for acute diarrhoeas. Bulk-forming drugs, such as ispaghula, methylcellulose, and sterculia (section 1.6.1) are useful in controlling diarrhoea associated with diverticular disease.
