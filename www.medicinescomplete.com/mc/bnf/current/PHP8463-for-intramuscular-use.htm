HEPATITIS B IMMUNOGLOBULIN

Indications

 

prophylaxis against hepatitis B infection

Cautions

 

IgA deficiency; interference with live virus vaccines see under Normal Immunoglobulin.

Side-effects

 

injection site reactions; less frequently, buccal ulceration, glossitis, abdominal pain, chest pain, dyspnoea, anaphylaxis, tremor, dizziness, headache, arthralgia; for side-effects associated with intravenous immunoglobulin, see section 14.5.1

Dose

  • See under preparations and see also notes above

Sub-sections

For intramuscular use

  • Hepatitis B Immunoglobulin
  • For intravenous use
    • Hepatect®CP
  • For subcutaneous use
    • Zutectra®
