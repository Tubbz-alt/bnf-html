    Piperaquine with Artenimol

    Piperaquine with Artenimol has the following interaction information:

    Note: Piperaquine has a long half-life; there is a potential for drug interactions to occur for up to 3 months after treatment has been stopped

    Amiodarone

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with amiodarone (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Note: Amiodarone has a long half-life; there is a potential for drug interactions to occur for several weeks (or even months) after treatment with it has been stopped

    Antidepressants

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with antidepressants

    Antifungals, Imidazole

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with imidazoles (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Antifungals, Triazole

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with triazoles (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Arsenic Trioxide

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with arsenic trioxide (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Carbamazepine

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with carbamazepine

    Disopyramide

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with disopyramide (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Domperidone

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with domperidone (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Droperidol

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with droperidol (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Grapefruit Juice

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with grapefruit juice

    Haloperidol

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with haloperidol (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Macrolides

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with macrolides (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Note: See also Telithromycin

    Methadone

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with methadone (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Mizolastine

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with mizolastine (possible risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Phenobarbital

    Phenothiazines

    Vinflunine

    Vinorelbine

    manufacturer of piperaquine with artenimol advises avoid concomitant use with vinorelbine

    Piperaquine with Artenimol belongs to Piperaquine but Piperaquine has no interactions information.

    Piperaquine with Artenimol belongs to Antimalarials and will have the following interaction information:

    Artemether with Lumefantrine

    avoidance of antimalarials advised by manufacturer of artemether with lumefantrine

    Histamine

    avoidance of antimalarials advised by manufacturer of histamine

    Typhoid Vaccine (oral)

    antimalarials inactivate oral typhoid vaccine—see under Typhoid vaccines, section 14.4
