CEFRADINE

(Cephradine)

Additional information interactions (Cefradine).

Indications

 

see under Cefaclor; surgical prophylaxis

Cautions

 

see under Cefaclor; interactions: Appendix 1 (cephalosporins)

Contra-indications

 

see under Cefaclor

Renal impairment

 

use half normal dose if eGFR 5–20 mL/minute/1.73 m2; use one-quarter normal dose if eGFR less than 5 mL/minute/1.73 m2

Pregnancy

 

see under Cefaclor

Breast-feeding

 

see under Cefaclor

Side-effects

 

see under Cefaclor

Dose

 

250–500 mg every 6 hours or 0.5–1 g every 12 hours; up to 1 g every 6 hours in severe infections; child 7–12 years, 25–50 mg/kg daily in 2–4 divided doses

Sub-sections

  • Cefradine (Non-proprietary)

    Capsules, cefradine 250 mg, net price 20-cap pack = £3.08; 500 mg, 20-cap pack = £5.62. Label: 9
    Brands include Nicef®

    Dental prescribing on NHS

     

    Cefradine Capsules may be prescribed
