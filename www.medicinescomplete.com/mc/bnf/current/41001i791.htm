Saquinavir

Saquinavir has the following interaction information:

Alfentanilincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with alfentanil—avoid concomitant use
Amiodaroneincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with amiodarone—avoid concomitant useAmiodarone has a long half-life; there is a potential for drug interactions to occur for several weeks (or even months) after treatment with it has been stopped
Antidepressants, Tricyclicincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with tricyclics—avoid concomitant use
Antifungals, Imidazoleplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly increased by imidazoles
Antifungals, Triazoleplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly increased by triazoles
Apixabanavoidance of saquinavir advised by manufacturer of apixaban
Aripiprazole saquinavir possibly inhibits metabolism of aripiprazole (reduce dose of aripiprazole)
Artemether with Lumefantrinecaution with saquinavir advised by manufacturer of artemether/lumefantrine
Atazanavirincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with atazanavir—avoid concomitant use
Atorvastatinpossible increased risk of myopathy when saquinavir given with atorvastatin
Cabazitaxelavoidance of saquinavir advised by manufacturer of cabazitaxel
Carbamazepineplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly reduced by carbamazepine
Ciclosporinplasma concentration of both drugs increased when saquinavir given with ciclosporin
Cimetidineplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly increased by cimetidine
Clarithromycinincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with clarithromycin—avoid concomitant use
Clozapineincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with clozapine—avoid concomitant useAvoid concomitant use of clozapine with drugs that have a substantial potential for causing agranulocytosis
Dapsoneincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with dapsone—avoid concomitant use
Darifenacinavoidance of saquinavir advised by manufacturer of darifenacin
Darunavir saquinavir reduces plasma concentration of darunavir
Dexamethasoneplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly reduced by dexamethasone
Disopyramideincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with disopyramide—avoid concomitant use
Dronedaroneincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with dronedarone—avoid concomitant use
Efavirenzplasma concentration of saquinavir significantly reduced by efavirenz
Eplerenone saquinavir increases plasma concentration of eplerenone (reduce dose of eplerenone)
Ergotamine and Methysergideincreased risk of ergotism when saquinavir given with ergotamine and methysergide—avoid concomitant use
Erythromycinincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with erythromycin—avoid concomitant useInteractions do not apply to small amounts of erythromycin used topically
Esomeprazoleplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly increased by esomeprazole—manufacturer of saquinavir advises avoid concomitant use
Everolimus saquinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of everolimus—manufacturer of everolimus advises avoid concomitant use
Fentanylincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with fentanyl—avoid concomitant use
Fesoterodinemanufacturer of fesoterodine advises dose reduction when saquinavir given with fesoterodine—consult fesoterodine product literature
Flecainideincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with flecainide—avoid concomitant use
Haloperidolincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with haloperidol—avoid concomitant use
Indinavirplasma concentration of saquinavir increased by indinavir
Ketoconazoleplasma concentration of saquinavir increased by ketoconazole
Lansoprazoleplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly increased by lansoprazole—manufacturer of saquinavir advises avoid concomitant use
Lapatinibavoidance of saquinavir advised by manufacturer of lapatinib
Lidocaineincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with lidocaine—avoid concomitant useInteractions less likely when lidocaine used topically
Lopinavirincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with lopinavir—avoid concomitant useIn combination with ritonavir as Kaletra® (ritonavir is present to inhibit lopinavir metabolism and increase plasma-lopinavir concentration)—see also Ritonavir
Maraviroc saquinavir increases plasma concentration of maraviroc (consider reducing dose of maraviroc)
Methadoneincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with methadone—avoid concomitant use
Midazolam saquinavir increases plasma concentration of midazolam (risk of prolonged sedation—avoid concomitant use of oral midazolam)
Mizolastineincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with mizolastine—avoid concomitant use
Nelfinavirplasma concentration of saquinavir increased by nelfinavir—manufacturer of saquinavir advises avoid concomitant use
Omeprazoleplasma concentration of saquinavir increased by omeprazole—manufacturer of saquinavir advises avoid concomitant use
Pantoprazoleplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly increased by pantoprazole—manufacturer of saquinavir advises avoid concomitant use
Pazopanibavoidance of saquinavir advised by manufacturer of pazopanib
Pentamidine Isetionateincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with pentamidine isetionate—avoid concomitant use
Phenobarbitalplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly reduced by phenobarbital
Phenothiazinesincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with phenothiazines—avoid concomitant use
Phenytoinplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly reduced by phenytoin
Pimozide saquinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of pimozide (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias—avoid concomitant use)
Propafenoneincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with propafenone—avoid concomitant use
Quetiapine saquinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of quetiapine—manufacturer of quetiapine advises avoid concomitant use
Quinineincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with quinine—avoid concomitant use
Rabeprazoleplasma concentration of saquinavir possibly increased by rabeprazole—manufacturer of saquinavir advises avoid concomitant use
Ranolazine saquinavir possibly increases plasma concentration of ranolazine—manufacturer of ranolazine advises avoid concomitant use
Rifabutin saquinavir increases plasma concentration of rifabutin (also plasma concentration of saquinavir reduced)
Rifampicinplasma concentration of saquinavir significantly reduced by rifampicin , also risk of hepatotoxicity—avoid concomitant use
Ritonavirplasma concentration of saquinavir increased by ritonavir
Rivaroxabanavoidance of saquinavir advised by manufacturer of rivaroxaban
Rosuvastatinpossible increased risk of myopathy when saquinavir given with rosuvastatin—manufacturer of rosuvastatin advises avoid concomitant use
Sildenafilincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with sildenafil—avoid concomitant use
Simvastatinincreased risk of myopathy when saquinavir given with simvastatin (avoid concomitant use)
Sotalolincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with sotalol—avoid concomitant use
St John's Wortplasma concentration of saquinavir reduced by St John's wort—avoid concomitant use
Tacrolimus saquinavir increases plasma concentration of tacrolimus (consider reducing dose of tacrolimus)Interactions do not generally apply to tacrolimus used topically; risk of facial flushing and skin irritation with alcohol consumption (see section 13.5.3) does not apply to tacrolimus taken systemically
Tadalafilincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with tadalafil—avoid concomitant use
Telithromycinavoidance of concomitant saquinavir in severe renal and hepatic impairment advised by manufacturer of telithromycin
Tipranavirplasma concentration of saquinavir reduced by tipranavir
Tolterodineavoidance of saquinavir advised by manufacturer of tolterodine
Trazodoneincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with trazodone—avoid concomitant use
Vardenafilincreased risk of ventricular arrhythmias when saquinavir given with vardenafil—avoid concomitant use
Warfarin saquinavir possibly enhances anticoagulant effect of warfarin

Saquinavir belongs to Antivirals but Antivirals has no interactions information.
