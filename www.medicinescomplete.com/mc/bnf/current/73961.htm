RALOXIFENE HYDROCHLORIDE

Additional information interactions (Raloxifene).

Indications

 

treatment and prevention of postmenopausal osteoporosis

Cautions

 

risk factors for venous thromboembolism (discontinue if prolonged immobilisation); risk factors for stroke; breast cancer (see notes above); history of oestrogen-induced hypertriglyceridaemia (monitor serum triglycerides); avoid in acute porphyria (section 9.8.2); interactions: Appendix 1 (raloxifene)

Contra-indications

 

history of venous thromboembolism, undiagnosed uterine bleeding, endometrial cancer, cholestasis

Hepatic impairment

 

avoid

Renal impairment

 

caution in mild to moderate impairment; avoid in severe impairment

Side-effects

 

hot flushes, leg cramps, peripheral oedema, influenza-like symptoms; less commonly venous thromboembolism, thrombophlebitis; rarely rashes, gastro-intestinal disturbances, hypertension, arterial thromboembolism, headache (including migraine), breast discomfort, thrombocytopenia

Dose

 

60 mg once daily
