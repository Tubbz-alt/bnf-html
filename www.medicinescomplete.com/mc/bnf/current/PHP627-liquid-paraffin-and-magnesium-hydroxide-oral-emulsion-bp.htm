    MAGNESIUM SALTS

    Additional information

     

    interactions (Magnesium Salts (oral)).

    Indications

     

    see under preparations below

    Cautions

     

    elderly and debilitated; see also notes above; interactions: Appendix 1 (antacids)

    Contra-indications

     

    acute gastro-intestinal conditions

    Hepatic impairment

     

    avoid in hepatic coma if risk of renal failure

    Renal impairment

     

    avoid or reduce dose; increased risk of toxicity

    Side-effects

     

    colic

    Dose

    • See preparations

    Sub-sections

    • Magnesium hydroxide
      • Magnesium Hydroxide Mixture, BP

    Magnesium hydroxide with liquid paraffin

    Liquid Paraffin and Magnesium Hydroxide Oral Emulsion, BP

    Oral emulsion, 25% liquid paraffin in aqueous suspension containing 6% hydrated magnesium oxide

    Dose constipation, 5–20 mL when required

    Note

     

    Liquid paraffin and magnesium hydroxide preparations on sale to the public include: Milpar®
