ACICLOVIR

(Acyclovir)

Indications

 

local treatment of herpes simplex infections

Side-effects

 

local irritation and inflammation, superficial punctate keratopathy; rarely blepharitis; very rarely hypersensitivity reactions including angioedema

Dose

 

Apply 5 times daily (continue for at least 3 days after complete healing)
