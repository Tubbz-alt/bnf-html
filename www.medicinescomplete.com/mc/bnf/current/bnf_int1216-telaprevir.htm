    Telaprevir

    Telaprevir has the following interaction information:

    Alfuzosin

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with alfuzosin

    Amiodarone

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with amiodarone (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Note: Amiodarone has a long half-life; there is a potential for drug interactions to occur for several weeks (or even months) after treatment with it has been stopped

    Amlodipine

    telaprevir increases plasma concentration of amlodipine (consider reducing dose of amlodipine)

    Apixaban

    avoidance of telaprevir advised by manufacturer of apixaban

    Atazanavir

    plasma concentration of telaprevir possibly reduced by atazanavir, also plasma concentration of atazanavir possibly increased

    Atorvastatin

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with atorvastatin

    Bosentan

    plasma concentration of telaprevir possibly reduced by bosentan, also plasma concentration of bosentan possibly increased

    Budesonide

    telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of inhaled and intranasal budesonide

    Carbamazepine

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with carbamazepine

    Ciclosporin

    plasma concentration of both drugs increased when telaprevir given with ciclosporin (reduce dose of ciclosporin)

    Clarithromycin

    plasma concentration of both drugs possibly increased when telaprevir given with clarithromycin (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Colchicine

    telaprevir possibly increases risk of colchicine toxicity—suspend or reduce dose of colchicine (avoid concomitant use in hepatic or renal impairment)

    Dabigatran Etexilate

    telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of dabigatran etexilate

    Darunavir

    avoid concomitant use of telaprevir with darunavir

    Dexamethasone

    plasma concentration of telaprevir possibly reduced by dexamethasone

    Digoxin

    telaprevir increases plasma concentration of digoxin

    Diltiazem

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with diltiazem

    Disopyramide

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with disopyramide (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Domperidone

    telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of domperidone—manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use

    Efavirenz

    plasma concentration of telaprevir reduced by efavirenz—increase dose of telaprevir

    Ergot Alkaloids

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with ergot alkaloids

    Erythromycin

    plasma concentration of both drugs possibly increased when telaprevir given with erythromycin (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Note: Interactions do not apply to small amounts of erythromycin used topically

    Ethinylestradiol

    telaprevir possibly reduces plasma concentration of ethinylestradiol—manufacturer of telaprevir advises additional contraceptive precautions

    Felodipine

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with felodipine

    Flecainide

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with flecainide (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Fluticasone

    telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of inhaled and intranasal fluticasone

    Fosamprenavir

    manufacturers advise avoid concomitant use of telaprevir with fosamprenavir

    Note: Fosamprenavir is a prodrug of amprenavir

    Itraconazole

    telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of itraconazole

    Ketoconazole

    plasma concentration of both drugs possibly increased when telaprevir given with ketoconazole (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Lidocaine

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with intravenous lidocaine

    Note: Interactions less likely when lidocaine used topically

    Lopinavir

    manufacturers advise avoid concomitant use of telaprevir with lopinavir

    Note: In combination with ritonavir as Kaletra® (ritonavir is present to inhibit lopinavir metabolism and increase plasma-lopinavir concentration)—see also Ritonavir

    Methadone

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with methadone (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Midazolam

    telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of midazolam (risk of prolonged sedation—avoid concomitant use of oralmidazolam)

    Nicardipine

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with nicardipine

    Nifedipine

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with nifedipine

    Phenobarbital

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with phenobarbital

    Phenytoin

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with phenytoin

    Pimozide

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with pimozide

    Posaconazole

    telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of posaconazole (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Propafenone

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with propafenone (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Quetiapine

    telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of quetiapine—manufacturer of quetiapine advises avoid concomitant use

    Rifabutin

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with rifabutin

    Rifampicin

    plasma concentration of telaprevir significantly reduced by rifampicin—avoid concomitant use

    Ritonavir

    plasma concentration of telaprevir possibly reduced by ritonavir

    Ruxolitinib

    manufacturer of ruxolitinib advises dose reduction when telaprevir given with ruxolitinib—consult ruxolitinib product literature

    Salmeterol

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with salmeterol (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Sildenafil

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with sildenafil

    Simvastatin

    manufacturers advise avoid concomitant use of telaprevir with simvastatin

    Sirolimus

    plasma concentration of both drugs increased when telaprevir given with sirolimus (reduce dose of sirolimus)

    Sotalol

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with sotalol (risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    St John's Wort

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with St John's wort

    Tacrolimus

    plasma concentration of both drugs increased when telaprevir given with tacrolimus (reduce dose of tacrolimus)

    Note: Interactions do not generally apply to tacrolimus used topically; risk of facial flushing and skin irritation with alcohol consumption (see section 13.5.3) does not apply to tacrolimus taken systemically

    Tadalafil

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with high doses of tadalafil—consult product literature

    Telithromycin

    plasma concentration of both drugs possibly increased when telaprevir given with telithromycin (increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Tenofovir

    telaprevir increases plasma concentration of tenofovir

    Trazodone

    telaprevir possibly increases plasma concentration of trazodone

    Vardenafil

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises avoid concomitant use with vardenafil

    Verapamil

    manufacturer of telaprevir advises caution with verapamil

    Voriconazole

    telaprevir possibly affects plasma concentration of voriconazole (possible increased risk of ventricular arrhythmias)

    Warfarin

    telaprevir possibly affects plasma concentration of warfarin

    Telaprevir belongs to Antivirals but Antivirals has no interactions information.
