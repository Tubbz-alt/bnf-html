    ETHERIFIED STARCH

    A starch composed of more than 90% of amylopectin that has been etherified with hydroxyethyl groups; the terms tetrastarch, pentastarch, and hetastarch reflect the degree of etherification

    Indications

     

    low blood volume

    Cautions

     

    see notes above; children

    Hepatic impairment

     

    use with caution in severe impairment

    Renal impairment

     

    use with caution in mild to moderate impairment; avoid in severe impairment

    Side-effects

     

    see notes above; also pruritus, raised serum amylase

    Dose

    • See under preparations below
