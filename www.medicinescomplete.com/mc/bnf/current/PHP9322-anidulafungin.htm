Anidulafungin

(Ecalta®)

Intermittent in Glucose 5% or Sodium chloride 0.9%

Reconstitute each 100 mg with 30 mL water for injections, allow up to 5 minutes for reconstitution; dilute dose in infusion fluid to a concentration of 770 micrograms/mL; give at a rate not exceeding 1.1 mg/minute

Note

 

Follow product information if using stock supplied with ethanol solvent
