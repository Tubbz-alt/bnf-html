TRIPTORELIN

Indications

 

endometriosis; precocious puberty; reduction in size of uterine fibroids; male hypersexuality with severe sexual deviation; advanced prostate cancer (section 8.3.4.2)

Cautions

 

see notes above

Contra-indications

 

see notes above

Pregnancy

 

see notes above

Breast-feeding

 

see notes above

Side-effects

 

see notes above; also gastro-intestinal disturbances; in precocious puberty, withdrawal bleeding in females may occur in the first month of treatment; asthenia
