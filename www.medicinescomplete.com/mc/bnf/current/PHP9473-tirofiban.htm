Tirofiban

(Aggrastat®)

Continuous in Glucose 5% or Sodium chloride 0.9%

Withdraw 50 mL infusion fluid from 250-mL bag and replace with 50 mL tirofiban concentrate (250 micrograms/mL) to give a final concentration of 50 micrograms/mL
