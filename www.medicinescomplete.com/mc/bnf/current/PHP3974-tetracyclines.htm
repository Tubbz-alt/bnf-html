Tetracyclines

Doxycycline (section 5.1.3) is used for the prophylaxis of malaria in areas of widespread mefloquine or chloroquine resistance. Doxycycline is also used as an alternative to mefloquine or Malarone® (for details, see specific recommendations by country).

Doxycycline is also used as an adjunct to quinine in the treatment of falciparum malaria (for details see Falciparum Malaria (Treatment)).
