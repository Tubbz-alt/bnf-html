LATANOPROST

Indications

 

raised intra-ocular pressure in open-angle glaucoma; ocular hypertension

Cautions

 

see notes above; also history of herpetic keratitis (avoid in active herpes simplex keratitis); cataract surgery

Pregnancy

 

manufacturer advises avoid

Breast-feeding

 

may be present in milk—manufacturer advises avoid

Side-effects

 

see notes above; also reported herpetic keratitis, iris cyst

Dose

 

Apply once daily, preferably in the evening
